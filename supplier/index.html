Partner Requirements

To maintain Coolth's reputation for quality emergency service, all partners must meet our stringent standards.

RLNZ Certification

Current Refrigerant License New Zealand certification required for all refrigerant handling. Must maintain compliance with RLNZ regulations and safety standards.

Professional Qualifications

Trade qualification in refrigeration/air conditioning, or equivalent experience. Commercial refrigeration experience highly valued.

Emergency Availability

Ability to respond to emergency callouts within 30-60 minutes. After-hours and weekend availability for premium rate work.

Insurance & Compliance

Public liability insurance minimum $2M, professional indemnity cover, and full health & safety compliance.

Equipment & Tools

Full refrigeration toolkit including gauges, vacuum pump, leak detector, temperature loggers, and refrigerant recovery equipment.

Vehicle Requirements

Reliable service vehicle with secure refrigerant storage, proper ventilation, and professional presentation.

Technology Setup

Smartphone for dispatch system, GPS tracking, photo documentation, and real-time customer communication.

MPI Knowledge

Understanding of MPI food safety requirements for commercial work. Ability to maintain cold chain compliance.
